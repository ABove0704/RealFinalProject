The Jounrey to Weight Loss - Directions: Hello! Welcome to my short film slideshow about "The Journey to Weight Loss." Each page consists of different parts of my film. Charlie goes through his phone and sees photos of himself overweight. He gets upset and starts to cry. He pushes himself out of bed to go workout. He goes to the stairs at the Kensico Dam in Westchester. Over the past, six months Charlie had lost over twenty pounds and is continuing to cook for himself every night and working out everyday.
